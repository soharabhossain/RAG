Sherlock Holmes lived at 221B Baker Street in London, England. This address is one of the most iconic addresses in literature and has become synonymous with the character of Sherlock Holmes.

His loyal companion and close friend was Dr. John H. Watson. Dr. Watson was a former army doctor who served in Afghanistan before meeting Holmes. He became Holmes's flatmate and chronicler of his adventures. Watson narrated most of the stories featuring Sherlock Holmes and provided valuable insights into Holmes's methods and personality.

Together, Sherlock Holmes and Dr. John Watson formed one of the most famous detective duos in literary history, with Holmes's brilliant mind and deductive skills complemented by Watson's practicality and loyalty. Their adventures solving mysteries and crimes captivated readers and made them beloved characters in the world of literature.
